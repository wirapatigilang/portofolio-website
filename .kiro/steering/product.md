# Product Overview

Personal portfolio website for **Gilang Wirapati** showcasing two distinct professional domains:

1. **Software Engineering** — developer skills, projects, and tech stack
2. **Creative Work** — Photography, Videography, and Video Editing

The site serves as a professional representation to potential clients, recruiters, and collaborators. Both domains are independently navigable but unified under a single personal identity.

## Key Pages

- **Home (`/`)** — Hero section, domain entry points, short bio, CTA to Contact
- **Software Engineering (`/software`)** — Tech stack, filterable project cards with detail modals
- **Creative Work (`/creative`)** — Photo gallery with lightbox, video portfolio with embed player
- **About (`/about`)** — Full biography, career/education timeline, achievements, CV download
- **Contact (`/contact`)** — Contact form (via Resend), social links, mailto link

## Content Strategy

All portfolio content (projects, photos, videos, owner data) is stored as **static TypeScript data files** in `src/data/`. There is no CMS — the owner updates content directly in the codebase.

## Target Audience

- Technical recruiters evaluating software engineering skills
- Potential clients for photography, videography, or video editing services
- Collaborators seeking creative or technical partnerships
